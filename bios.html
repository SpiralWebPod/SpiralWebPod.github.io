Daedalus (he/they)
Narrative Arc, Social Media

Daedalus is an indistinct form upon the landscape. A trick of the light.

A vague assemblage of colors and textures, He seems to ever be moving in and out of focus. He is a flood of words, the type that only holds meaning when in the midst of a dream.

Daedalus is as much a notion as anything. Three spirit-bodies in a fleshcoat.

Daedalus isn't his actual name. Daedalus is his real name.

Socials

@MindofDaedalus on Twitter & Bluesky, @Daedalus@Pagan.Plus on Mastodon, Daedalus.prism@gmail.com, Daedalus.the.witch on Discord

Ember (they/she)
Content Editor, Narrative Arc

Originally from Erie lands (Turtle Island), Ember has been a practicing Pagan Witch for over 20 years, and a Reclaiming Witch for 10! They've been creating online content about modern Paganism and Witchcraft since 2009 (Cara Mia @cutewitch772 on YouTube) and have taught in-person and online.

With a theatre arts background, Ember's magic blends their love of story-telling, meaning-making, and creating ritual. They have been a Ritualist, Path Facilitator, Tech Witch, and Organizer at various Reclaiming Witchcamps.

As part of the Spiral Web Podcast team, Ember has taken many notes in meetings and interviews, and is excited to lend the skills they've developed over a decade and a half of making video content to editing our Patreon exclusive content, as well as the final podcast that our audience will enjoy. Learn more about what Ember is up to at CaraMiaWitch.com.

Jade (she/her)
Web Design

Jade is a Reclaiming witch living on Darug and Guringai country, in the watershed of Dyarubbin.

After discovering Reclaiming in 2018, she dove headfirst into the ecstatic experiences, learning much, and discovering how much more there was still to learn, forget, remember, and unlearn.

She loves to craft, whether it be with fibre, metal, wood, food, words, music, ritual, or digital bits. Finding patterns, systems, and messages of coherence amid chaos brings her joy and satisfaction, and she adores getting deep into the weeds and the details of anything and everything, teasing out threads of connection, meaning, nuance, and intricacy.
